About Us

Online Career Progression was founded by a group of career development experts who wanted to create a platform for professionals to advance their careers. It started as a small online initiative with a commitment to providing high-quality career advice and skill training programs. Our vision is to continue growing and expanding our reach while maintaining our core values of excellence, innovation, and client satisfaction.

Future Strategy

  • Technology Enhancement: Continuously improving our website and mobile app for a seamless and user-friendly experience.
  • Diversification: Expanding our training program catalogue to include more niche and emerging skills.
  • Sustainability: Strengthening our commitment to sustainability by promoting more eco-friendly practices and programs.
  • Community Engagement: Partnering with industry leaders and organisations to promote career involvement and support professional growth.
  • Global Expansion: Exploring opportunities for international expansion to bring our career development programs to a global audience.
